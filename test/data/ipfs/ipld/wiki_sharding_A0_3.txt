          <div id="mw-content-text" style="direction: ltr;"><h1 class="section-heading" tabindex="0" aria-haspopup="true" data-section-id="0">
  <span class="mw-headline" id="title_0"><i>Tonight We Ride</i></span>
</h1>
<div id="mf-section-0" class="mf-section-0" aria-pressed="true" aria-expanded="true">
  <p><i><b>Tonight We Ride</b></i> is the twelfth album by American singer-songwriter <a href="../Michael_Martin_Murphey" title="Michael Martin Murphey">Michael Martin Murphey</a> and his first for <a href="../Warner_Bros._Records" title="Warner Bros. Records" class="mw-redirect">Warner Bros. Records</a>. Released in 1986, the album was produced by <a href="../Jim_Ed_Norman" title="Jim Ed Norman">Jim Ed Norman</a> and contains guest performances by <a href="../Pam_Tillis" title="Pam Tillis">Pam Tillis</a>, <a href="../Reggie_Young" title="Reggie Young">Reggie Young</a>, <a href="../Mark_O&apos;Connor" title="Mark O&apos;Connor">Mark O&apos;Connor</a>, <a href="../Charlie_McCoy" title="Charlie McCoy">Charlie McCoy</a>, and <a href="../J.D._Souther" title="J.D. Souther" class="mw-redirect">J.D. Souther</a>. The album&apos;s title track was also its first single. The album peaked at number 46 on the <i><a href="../Billboard_(magazine)" title="Billboard (magazine)">Billboard</a></i> Top Country Albums chart.<span class="mw-ref reference" id="cite_ref-allmusic_2-0"><a href="../#cite_note-allmusic-2" style="counter-reset: mw-Ref 2;"><span class="mw-reflink-text">[2]</span></a></span><span class="mw-ref reference" id="cite_ref-discogs_3-0"><a href="../#cite_note-discogs-3" style="counter-reset: mw-Ref 3;"><span class="mw-reflink-text">[3]</span></a></span></p>
